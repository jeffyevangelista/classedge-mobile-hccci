What changed

Current issues

  • 1Subtitle crammed against title — only 4px gap; reads as one block.
  • 2192px dead zone (mb-48) — splits the screen into two visual halves with nothing in between.
  • 3Email button border is too faint — outline variant barely visible against the background.
  • 4Floating debug overlay — confirm this is dev-only and not shipped.

Proposed improvements

  • ASubtitle gets mt-2 (6px) — clearer hierarchy between title and tagline.
  • BStronger email button border — bumped to border-strong with a subtle elevation; still secondary but visible.
  • C"Or" divider — gives the email option a job and clarifies it's an alternative, not a redundant CTA.
  • •Single centered block — logo + auth stack live together via justify-center, no fixed margin. Scales well from small phones to tablets.
  • •Legal text pinned to bottom — unchanged, but breathes naturally without the dead zone pushing it.
