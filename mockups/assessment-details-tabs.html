Why tabs on phone:
  • Each section is fully readable — Instructions doesn't have to share its scroll viewport with Materials and Results.
  • Hero stays anchored — title, status, stats, and rules are always visible regardless of which tab is active.
  • CTA stays pinned — Start Assessment is always reachable without scrolling.
  • Counts in tab badges tell the student what's there before tapping (e.g. "Materials 3", "Results 2").
  • Rules merged into hero subtitle on phone (the separate meta line goes away) — saves a row.
  • Tablet keeps current inline layout — tabs would feel like artificial chrome when the screen has the space to show everything.
Trade-off:

Students need to tap to see Materials or Results. Mitigation: tab badges show counts (so they know there's content), and the default landing tab can be smart — Instructions for "fresh" assessments, Results for "completed" ones.
