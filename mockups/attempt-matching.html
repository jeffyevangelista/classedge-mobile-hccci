Specifics:
  • Cards: rounded-xl, px-3 py-3, matching MC and T/F.
  • Letter/number chip: w-7 h-7 rounded-xl square, bold (was small circle).
  • Paired state: 2pt accent border + 4-pt left strip + a small rounded-lg filled accent badge on the right showing the partner's chip label. The badge replaces today's "→ 2" text.
  • Selecting state (left tapped, no partner yet): same accent treatment + a pulsing ring (radial shadow) + an "PICK →" mini-label inline. The other column's available targets get a soft accent border so the student knows where to tap next.
  • Selection hint: small chip at the top of the matching block ("Tap a match on the right") — only renders while a left item is selected and unpaired.
  • Completion banner when every item is paired (success-soft, check icon, "All N pairs matched").
  • All colors via theme tokens (accent, accent-foreground, success, warning, default, border) — adapts to dark mode.
