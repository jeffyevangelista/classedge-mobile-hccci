Trade-offs

Option A — Material + counts

  • Low risk — same navigator, just adds count pills and a dot.
  • Keeps the swipe-between-tabs gesture for free.
  • Still feels like Material Design — visually distinct from the rest of the app's pill tabs.
  • Indicator underline can clip against the count pill on narrow screens.

Option B — Pill segmented

  • Unifies the tab vocabulary across the app (assessment-details, here, anywhere else).
  • Floats with breathing room — no full-width navigation chrome.
  • Slightly more work — custom tabBar render prop.
  • Loses the Material indicator slide animation (can add a CSS transform if needed).

Recommendation: Option B. One tab idiom across the app is the bigger win; the small swap in implementation is worth it.
