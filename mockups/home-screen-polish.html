What changed & why

  • Empty schedule card — gradient bg, tropical emoji, and a "Browse courses →" CTA. Turns the biggest dead pixel into a soft conversion point.
  • Up Next card — drops accent-soft fill in favor of a thinner outline. Stops echoing the (now reactivated) gradient card and steps back as supporting info.
  • Section icons — small color-coded icon next to each title (calendar / newspaper / bell) gives the screen rhythm without competing with content.
  • Announcement preview — adds a body snippet under the title and shows 2 entries so the section reads at glance, not as a stub.
  • Times include room — "4:51 PM · Room 204" is more useful than just the time and fills the card without padding.
  • No global color injection — accent stays blue; differentiation comes from layout and density, not from adding hues.
